Facility & Infastructure

Maintenance

One truck, one hundred uses, is the best way to describe our fleet of hydrovac trucks. To compliment our range of hydro excavation services using our hydrovac units, we also have a variety of facilities and infrastructure maintenance services. We can offer these services as requested, in an emergency with our 24-hour service, or as part of a regularly scheduled maintenance plan. Many of our clients opt for a maintenance plan to increase longevity and ensure peak performance of facilities and infrastructure. If you are interested in regularly scheduled maintenance or waste removals, contact us to speak with one of our team members. Learn more about our specific hydrovac and facilities maintenance services below.

Sewer Flushing & Catch Basin Cleaning

The versatile nature of our trucks, coupled with our experience, enables us to provide a full range of sewer, catch basin, storm drain, and manhole cleaning and flushing services. Using blasts of high pressure, our equipment can clean blocked and clogged storm and sanitary lines from items including sludge, organic debris, trash, oil, grease, and other deposits. Once the blockage is displaced, we are also able to remove the materials to an approved off-site location. Using our fleets' vacuum systems supported by a controlled high-pressure system with heated water, as required, we are also able to clean all types of sanitary and stormwater infrastructure.

Liquid Waste Removal (MOE Approved)

With over 50 years of experience in the sanitation business, we provide septic tank cleaning and liquid waste disposal. Using our fleets' vacuum systems supported by a controlled high-pressure system, with heated water if required, we're also able to clean all types of tanks safely and efficiently. We're Ministry of Environment approved and licensed, which ensures that we are always providing safe and efficient removal of all kinds of liquid waste.

Wet or Dry Industrial Cleaning

As part of our range of versatile services, we're experienced and able to offer a variety of cleaning and waste removal services for all industrial facilities. Utilizing our high power vacuum units and heated controlled high-pressure water systems we're able to tackle the cleanup, removal and transfer of materials including but not limited to oil, water, coolants, dust and sand. Our services also extend to spills and contaminated sites. We're Ministry of Environment approved and licensed, therefore providing safe and efficient removal and transfer of a wide variety of wet and dry materials.
