Ken Lapain & Sons LTD

Auto Body & Collision Repair

At Ken Lapain & Sons, we are a full-service, PPG & Dupont-certified commercial and heavy equipment body shop. Our team of three auto body and collision damage technicians are fully licensed and have over 30 years of experience in the industry. Our team, combined with our Essex County-based shop, allow us to provide Southwestern Ontario with a variety of auto body repair services, including: fiberglass repairs, SMC repairs, full frame repairs, frame stretching, boom repairs, bucket repairs, and more. We welcome insurance claims and free estimates are available upon request. Contact us today to learn more and to get started.

Body Shop

As a customer-first business, we make sure to only use the best materials and attain the proper training and certifications for our staff. We use PPG and Dupont products and our staff and facility are fully certified by each supplier. This ensures that we are always providing reliable, consistent service to our customers in every industry, regardless of the job type or size.

Fiberglass & SMC Repairs

Since we opened our repair shop 30 years ago, one of the specialties of our auto body team has been fiberglass and SMC repair. Fiberglass and SMC repair requires the proper tools, equipment, training, and experience to be done correctly. Our facility is fully-equipped to handle almost any fiberglass or SMC repair in Southwestern Ontario and our experienced and certified team members will restore your commercial or heavy-duty vehicle to pre-accident condition.

Full Frame Repair & Stretching

The body shop of our Essex County-based facility is equipped with a 20’ x 40’ in-floor frame rack. This industry-standard setup allows our experienced, licensed technicians to pull all collision damage repairs with confidence, restoring your vehicle to pre-accident condition.

Boom & Bucket Repairs

Our team’s 30+ years of experience in the commercial and heavy-duty truck body shop industry makes us uniquely positioned to offer boom and bucket repairs to businesses in Essex County and Southwestern Ontario. Boom and bucket repairs have become one of our shop’s many specialties and we are proud to be one of the premier service providers in the area. Our team will quickly and efficiently get your boom or bucket truck back to working condition to minimize downtime.
