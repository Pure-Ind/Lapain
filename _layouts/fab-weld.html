Full Service Welding Repair and Fabrication Division

Since our service division opened in the 1980s, we’ve been offering full-service welding, repair, and fabrication services to customers across Essex County and Southwestern Ontario. Our facility is fully-equipped to handle almost any size project. We perform on-site welding repairs, fabrication jobs, and installations. Large & Small

We Tackle All Jobs

At Ken Lapain & Sons, no job is too big or too small for our team of professional custom fabricators and welders. Our Essex County-based company is perfectly located to provide fabrication and welding services to most of Southwestern Ontario, be it on-site or in-house in our fully-equipped facility.

Custom Fabrication

Based in Essex County, we are fully-equipped to perform almost any custom fabrication services in Southwestern Ontario. Custom fabrication projects come with unique demands and having the knowledge and experience to meet those demands is key. Our fabrication division has been in business since 1980 and we’ve completed jobs of all shapes, sizes, and difficulties both in-house and on-site. We specialize in multiple types of welding, and most often work with steel, aluminum, and stainless steel.

Welding / Welding Repair

Our facility and team of professional welders are equipped to tackle all jobs, large and small. We specialize in multiple types of welding, including: gas metal arc welding, shielded metal arc welding, and TIG welding. We have experience in and are equipped for welding steel, aluminum, and stainless materials and can take on any project, in-house or on-site.
